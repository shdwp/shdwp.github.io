Mobile game production that I've worked on during the span of 2022-2023. It was a 3D strategy game, with some idle mechanics. The team consisted of around 40 people.

I was responsible for the coding of background exploration feature, in which players send out units to outside world, with the facing somechallenges along the way and bringing back loot.
